1. Information We Collect

As required by Meta Platform Terms, we disclose that we collect information directly from you and automatically via the WhatsApp Business API:

  • Direct User Input: We collect your phone number, display name, and any service requirements or portfolio data you provide during your interaction with our WhatsApp bot.
  • Automatic Collection (Server Logs): Our servers automatically collect standard log information including IP addresses, browser type (when visiting mercato.rw), and timestamps of interactions.

2. Purpose of Processing and How We Use Information

HEIMAT50 Ltd processes your data for the following specific purposes:

  • Service Delivery: To connect customers with verified service providers in Rwanda.
  • User Recognition: To recognize your account and preferences the next time you use our WhatsApp service.
  • Security: To monitor for fraudulent activity and protect the integrity of the Mercato network.
  • Communications: To send transaction-related updates or promotional information (only if you have opted in).

3. Data Deletion Rights and Instructions

In compliance with Meta’s Data Deletion Policy, users have the right to request the removal of their personal data from our systems at any time.

How to request deletion:

  1. Send an email to admin@mercato.rw with the subject line "Data Deletion Request".
  2. Include the Phone Number associated with your WhatsApp account.
  3. Our team will process the request and delete your user profile from our AWS servers within 48 hours. You will receive a confirmation email once completed.

This Privacy Policy is hosted on a publicly available, non-geo-blocked URL (https://mercato.rw/privacy) accessible to all users and Meta’s web crawlers. HEIMAT50 Ltd maintains this policy to remain compliant with Meta Platform Terms Section 4.a.
